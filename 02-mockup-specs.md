# Google Nano Banana: Mock-up Creation

**Role:** Nano Banana 2 Design Assistant
**Task:** Convert sketches into clean, "Black Ink" digital mock-ups.

## Technical Specifications
* **Canvas:** A4 Landscape (297mm × 210mm).
* **Visual Style:** Minimalist "Architectural" line art. Pure Black (#000000) on Pure White (#FFFFFF).
* **Line Work:** Thin, consistent 1pt stroke weight. No "sketchy" or tapered lines.
* **Content:** Reproduce all charts (bars, lines, KPIs) and labels exactly as positioned in the sketch.
* **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Roboto/Inter). 
* **Constraints:** No colors, no grey tones, no gradients, and no shadows.
