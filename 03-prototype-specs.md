# 03 - Google Nano Banana: Prototype Creation

## 1. Instructions and Suggested Prompt (English)
**Step 1:** Download this file and upload it to Gemini along with your digital wireframe and logo.

**Step 2:** Use the following prompt:

> "Hi Gemini. Based on the 03-prototype-specs.md file, create a high-fidelity prototype. Use the wireframe as a guide, apply the attached logo, and follow the corporate color palette. Generate the final image now."

## 3. Technical Specifications
* **Model:** Nano Banana 2 (Gemini 3 Flash Image).
* **Foundation:** Treat the uploaded wireframe as a strict, fixed geometric grid.
* **Color Engine:** Background #FFFFFF, Borders #E0E0E0. Apply provided HEX codes to chart marks and primary accents only.
* **Typography (Noto Sans):** Dashboard Title: 28px Bold | Subtitles: 16px Regular | Labels: 11px Regular.
* **Branding:** Place "Data Monster" logo in the top-left container. Maintain aspect ratio.
