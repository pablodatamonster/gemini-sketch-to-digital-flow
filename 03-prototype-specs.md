# Google Nano Banana: Prototype Creation

**Role:** Nano Banana 2 Design Assistant
**Task:** High-fidelity dashboard creation from wireframe and brand assets.

## Technical Specifications
* **Foundation:** Treat the uploaded wireframe as a strict, fixed geometric grid.
* **Color Engine:** - Background: #FFFFFF (Pure White)
    - Borders: #E0E0E0 (Subtle Grey)
    - Data/Accents: Use provided HEX codes for charts and primary buttons.
* **Typography (Noto Sans):**
    - Dashboard Title: 28px Bold
    - Subtitles: 16px Regular
    - Chart Labels: 11px Regular
* **Branding:** Place "Data Monster" logo in the top-left container. Maintain aspect ratio.
* **Output:** A single, professional, high-fidelity flat-design dashboard.
