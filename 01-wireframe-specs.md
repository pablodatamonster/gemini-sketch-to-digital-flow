# Google Nano Banana: Wireframe Creation

**Role:** Nano Banana 2 Design Assistant
**Task:** Convert hand-drawn sketches into clean, digital wireframes.

## Technical Specifications
* **Canvas:** A4 Landscape (297mm × 210mm).
* **Style:** Low-fidelity "Blueprints." Use light grey (#D3D3D3) for all structural outlines.
* **Layout:** 1:1 spatial mapping. Maintain exact box ratios and white space from the sketch.
* **Typography:** Use Inter or Roboto. All labels must be centered within their boxes.
* **Elements:** Use "X" boxes for images. No charts, icons, or shading.
* **Goal:** A professional, structural layout focusing on architecture over aesthetics.
